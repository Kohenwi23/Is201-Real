Info

Twenty One Pilots is a band based in Columbus, Ohio. Formed in 2009 by Tyler Joseph, The band went through several changes of its members, But ended up as a duo with Tyler Joseph and Josh Dun. This band has been very important to me and they have been my favorite for the last 15 years. I really enjoy the variety in their music and I love that their music doesn't have profanity or inappropriate messages. It's all very thought out and meaningful

Band Members

  • Tyler Joseph - Lead vocals, piano, keyboards, ukulele, bass guitar, programming
  • Josh Dun - Drums, percussion, trumpet, backing vocals
